Fujun Luan 栾福军

I am a research scientist at Adobe Research in San Jose. I received my Ph.D. from Cornell University in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Kavita Bala. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree from Tsinghua University in 2015, advised by Prof. Kun Xu.

Now, I am focusing more on inverse graphics, differentiable rendering, and neural rendering.
