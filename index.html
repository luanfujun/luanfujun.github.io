Fujun Luan

I am currently a Research Scientist II at Adobe Research in San Jose. I received my Ph.D. from Cornell University in August 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Kavita Bala. Before that, I obtained my bachelor degree from Tsinghua University in 2015, working with Prof. Kun Xu.

My research interests are mainly in computer graphics and the affiliated parts of 3D vision, including: physically based rendering, inverse rendering, neural rendering, 3D reconstruction, 3D generation and neural relighting.
